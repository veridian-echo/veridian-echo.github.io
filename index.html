The Start of Forever

"Grow old along with me; the best is yet to be."

These words are a quiet tribute to the joy and love you’ve woven into my life—a simple reminder of how much you mean to me..

No firework moments, nothing staged,
Just hearts that learnt to be engaged..
In every message, every sound,
A piece of you I somehow found.

Side by side, the notes unfold
Shared adventures, futures bold.
No epic spark, no shooting star
Just quiet trust in who we are.

No fireworks marked our first hello,
Just woven fate and gentle flow.
Two names aligned by choice above-
In time, through grace, we grew to love.

With all the words I never sent,
