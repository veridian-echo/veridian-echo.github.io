The Start of Forever

"Grow old along with me; the best is yet to be."

Dearest love, this card is filled with the joy you've woven into my life, a quiet tribute to the love that continually brightens my world.

Bass drops low, my heartbeat speeds,
You glitch my code with playful deeds.
From concert halls to game controls,
You’re the player who steals my soul.

Now Brahms plays soft while dinners steam,
You win again in pixel dreams.
A symphony, a player two,
In every note, I find more you.

No fireworks marked our first hello,
Just woven fate and gentle flow.
Two names aligned by choice above--
In time, through grace, we grew to love.

Yours Forever,
Guru Prasad
